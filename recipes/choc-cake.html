Chocolate Sponge Cake

A delicous light tea time treat to share with friends and family

Chocolate Sponge Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 cup White Sugar
  • 2 1/2 Tbsp boiling water
  • 1 Tbsp Golden Syrup
  • 2 tsp cocoa
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 125gms cornflour
  • 1 heaped tsp baking powder
  • 1 heaped Tbsp flour

How to Make Chocolate Sponge Cake

  1. Separate eggs. Beat egg whites until thick. Add yolks and beat again
  2. Dissolve sugar in boiling water, gradually add to eggs along with the golden syrup and beat well for at least 3 minutes.
  3. Sift together all the dry ingredients and fold into mixture.
  4. Turn into 2 deep lightly greased and cornfloured sponge tins, at least 7cm deep.
  5. Bake at 190Â°C for 25 mins or until cooked. Cool for a few minutes in tins before turning out on to a wire rack
